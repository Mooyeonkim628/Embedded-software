For power pins, 3V and GND were used.
For GPIO, 14, 15, 32 and 33 were used.
Pin 32 was connected to button as input, with GND and 3V.
Pin 33 was connceted to mal-male jumper wire as sensor.
Pin 14 was connected to red led as output. 
Pin 15 was connected to green led as output that has sensor as input.
Both led and button have pull-down resistor.
