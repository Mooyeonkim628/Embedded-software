For power pins, 3V and GND were used.
For GPIO & Analog pins, 14, 32 and 34 were used.
Pin 32 was connected to button as input, with GND and 3V.
Pin 34 was connceted to potentiometer's pin#2 as analog input. 3V and GND were connected to pin#1 and pin#3.
Pin 14 was connected to led as PWM output. This pin was connected to cathode and GND was connected to anode.
